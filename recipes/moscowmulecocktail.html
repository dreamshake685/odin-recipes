Moscow Mule Cocktail

Description

This cocktail's origin has nothing to do with Moscow or mules, but after a few sips of this vodka, ginger beer, and lime concoction, no will care.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish
